Download latest version of AutoIT: https://www.autoitscript.com/site/autoit/downloads/
Recommended version: v3.3.14.5

How to run:
1. Right click on msl-bot.au3
2. Select 'Run Script (x86)'

How to compile an executable:
1. Right click on msl-bot.au3
2. Select 'Compile Script'

*Note: Add the MSLBot folder as an exception in your antivirus. Some files such as the executable or the ImageSearchLibrary
may be seen as a false positive by your antivirus. If ImageSearchLibrary.dll is removed then some complications may arise.
